Save to Your Device

After setting up your BigOsoftwares, you’ll receive a text message with a link to your card. We recommend saving the card to your mobile device's home screen for easy access and quick sharing anytime, anywhere. Follow these instructions to save to your home screen on iOS and Android:

Android users:

  1. Open the Website: Launch your preferred web browser (e.g., Chrome) and go to the website you want to save.
  2. Access the Menu: Tap the three vertical dots (menu icon) in the upper-right corner of the browser.
  3. Select 'Add to Home Screen': From the menu, choose "Add to Home screen" or a similar option.
  4. Name Your Shortcut: Enter a name for the shortcut (this will be the name displayed on your home screen).
  5. Add the Shortcut: Tap "Add" or "Add Automatically" to place the website icon on your home screen. 6. Locate the Shortcut: You should now see the website icon on your home screen, which you can tap to quickly access the site.

iPhone/iOS users:

  1. Open Safari: Launch the Safari browser on your iOS device.
  2. Navigate to the Website: Enter the URL of the website you want to save.
  3. Tap the Share Button: Tap the share icon at the bottom of the screen (a square with an upward arrow).
  4. Select "Add to Home Screen": Scroll down and tap on "Add to Home Screen."
  5. Name Your Shortcut: Enter a name for the shortcut, which will appear on your home screen.
  6. Tap "Add": Tap "Add" in the top-right corner to create the shortcut.

Share

To share your BigOsoftwares, simply open your card and click the 'Share' button. You can then select from various sharing options like SMS, WhatsApp, Email, or Facebook. Alternatively, you can copy the link to your BigOsoftwares and share it as you would with any other web page.
